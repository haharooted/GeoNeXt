WAR DEPARTMENT,

Washington City, D. C., September 13, 1862.

WILLIAM M. LENTER,

San Francisco, Cal.:

If 1,000 men for the war are placed at Panama from California passage will be provided thence for them.

By order of Secretary of War:

C. P. BUCKINGHAM,

Brigadier-General and Assistant Adjutant-General.

(Copy to M. Jessup. Eugene Sullivan, and William D. Chaplin.) Fort Vancouver, Wash. Ter., September 13, 1862.
