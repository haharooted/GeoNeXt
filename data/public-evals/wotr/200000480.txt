HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE MISSOURI,
Saint Louis, Mo., April 12, 1864.

(Received 10.10 a.m., 13th.)

Lieutenant-General GRANT,

Washington, D. C.:

I can and will send all mules I can to Louisville. The reason why so much transportation is used it that we have so much hauling. For example, from Rolla to Springfield all supplies have to be wagoner 160 miles.

W. S. ROSECRANS,

Major-General.
