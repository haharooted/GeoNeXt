HDQRS. OWEN'S RIVER EXPEDITION, Numbers 11.
 Camp Independence, Cal., September 27, 1862.

I. The commanders of Companies D and I, Second Cavalry California Volunteers, will hold themselves in readiness to march hence with their respective companies and all their camp and garrison equiPAGEon Monday morning, September 29, 1862.

II. The commanders of said companies will see that their respective companies are provided with fifteen days' rations from September 30, 1862.

* * * * * * *

By order:

GEO. S. EVANS,

Lieutenant-Colonel Second Cavalry California Volunteers, Commanding
