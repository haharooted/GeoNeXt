HDQRS. DIST. OF MINN., DEPT. OF THE NORTHWEST,
Saint Paul, Minn., May 31, 1864.

Major General JOHN POPE,

Milwaukee:

GENERAL: Orders were dispatched 28th for the rendezvous of Sixth Regiment at Fort Snelling, as soon as possible. Being much scattered, several days will be required to assemble them. There will be no unnecessary delay.

H. H. SIBLEY,

Brigadier-General, Commanding.
