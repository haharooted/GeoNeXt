[Indorsement.]

HEADQUARTERS CENTRAL DISTRICT OF MISSOURI,
Warrensburg, May 31, 1864

Respectfully referred to Major-General Rosecrans commanding Department of the Missouri, for his information in connection with my letter to him of the 30th, inclosing reports of scouting in the Third and Fourth Sub-Districts.

E. B. BROWN,

Brigadier-General of Volunteers, Commanding.
