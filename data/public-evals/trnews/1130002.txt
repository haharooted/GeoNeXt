LAUREL COUNTY, Ky. (WKYT) - A man is dead as a result of a crash on Thursday morning in Laurel County.
The Laurel County Sheriff's Office says at about 7 a.m., a white-colored Chevrolet Cavalier was traveling eastbound on Blackwater Road when the vehicle went out of control in a curve. It hit an embankment and overturned several times.
The driver died at the scene. The sheriff's department identified the man as 63-year-old Donnie Ray Smith of London. According to deputies, Smith was the only person in the car.
