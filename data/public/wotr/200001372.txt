 HDQRS. 1ST DIV., 13TH ARMY CORPS, Numbers 13. Indianola, Tex., January 28, 1864.

I. Brigadier General Fitz Henry Warrent, commanding First Brigade, First Division, Thirteenth Army Corps, is hereby ordered in arrest for disobedience of orders and disrespect to the brigadier-general commanding the division.

II. Colonel David Shunk, Eighth Indiana Infantry, will at once assume command of the First Brigade, First Division, Thirteenth Army Corps.

By order of Brigadier General W. P. Benton:

B. WILSON,
Assistant Adjutant-General.
