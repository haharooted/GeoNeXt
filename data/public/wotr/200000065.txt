SPECIAL FIELD ORDERS,
 HDQRS. ARMY OF THE TENNESSEE, Numbers 4.
 Camp Sugar Valley, Ga., May 9, 1864.

Reveille will be beat throughout the camp at 4.30 a. m. to-morrow. The troops will be held in readiness to meet or repel any attack of the enemy.

By order of Major General James B. McPherson:

JAMES WILSON,

Lieutenant-Colonel and Acting Assistant Adjutant-General.
