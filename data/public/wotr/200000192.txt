MARCH 3, 1864 - Skirmishes at Jackson and near Baton Rouge, La.

Abstract from Record of Events on return of the Second Brigade, Cavalry Division, Department of the Gulf, for the month of March, 1864.

On the 3rd, a small force went to Jackson; had a skirmish at that place; killed 1 rebel and took 1 prisoner. In the afternoon the same party encountered a superior force, and in charging them lost 3 men prisoners. On the same day Lieutenant-Colonel Logan went to Baton Rouge; had a skirmish; killed 1 rebel and lost 3 men prisoners.
