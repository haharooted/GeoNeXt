HEADQUARTERS FIFTEENTH ARMY CORPS,
Near Big Shanty, June 28, 1864

GENERAL: I have the honor to report the following prisoners of war captured June 27, 1864, by the Fifteenth Army Corps; Captain, 1; lieutenants, 2; sergeants, 7; corporals, 4; privates, 73; total, 87.

I have the honor to be, general, your most obedient servant,

FRANK C. GILLETTE,

Captain and Provost-Marshal, Fifteenth Army Corps.

Major General JOHN A. LOGAN,

Commanding Fifteenth Army Corps.
