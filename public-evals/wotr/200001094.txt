HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE PACIFIC,
San Francisco, Cal., September 15, 1862.

Colonel FERRIS FORMAN,

Fourth Infantry California Volunteers,

Commanding Camp Latham, near Los Angeles, Cal.:

SIR: Immediately upon the reciept of this communication the department commander directs you to dispatch an express to Lieutenant Colonel George S. Evans, Second Cavalry, directing him to proceed with two companies to Visalia, there to await further orders.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

R. C. DRUM,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
