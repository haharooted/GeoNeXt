HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE PACIFIC.

San Francisco, September 29, 1862.

Respectfully referred to Colonel Babbitt and Captain Kellogg, who will see that the troops at Owen's River and Visalia are properly supplied forthwith. Such articles used for winter campaign (as gloves, &c.) company to the extent of supplying that company.

By order of Major-General Wright:

R. C. DRUM,
Assistant Adjutant-General.
