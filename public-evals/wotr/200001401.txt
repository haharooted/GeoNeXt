HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE MISSOURI,
Saint Louis, Mo., January 27, 1864.

Captain C. G. LAURANT,

Fort Smith, Ark.:

By a recent order from War Department, the Department of Arkansas is created, Major-General Steele commanding all troops in the State of Arkansas except those forming the garrison of Fort Smith. Make returns to General Steele.

O. D. GREENE,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
