FIELD ORDERS,
 HDQRS. DEPARTMENT OF THE GULF, Numbers 21.
 Grand Ecore, La., April 16, 1864.

1. In pursuance of extract 1 of Special Orders, Numbers 130, from headquarters of the Army, Adjutant-General' Office, dated March department, and will in accordance therewith repair via New Orleans Army.

2. Brigadier General A. J. Smith, commanding detachment Sixteenth and Seventeenth Army Corps, will at once disembark the troops from all transports in his charge, the transports so discharged to be turned over to Lieutenant-Colonel Chandler, chief quartermaster, Nineteenth Army Corps, for service in the quartermaster's department, this transfer to be temporary and with the consent of General Smith.

* * * *

By command of Major-General Banks:

GEO. B. DRAKE,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
