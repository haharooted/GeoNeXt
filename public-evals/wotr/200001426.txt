SPECIAL ORDERS,
 HDQRS. DETACH. 16TH AND 17TH A. C., Numbers 22.
 On Steamer Clara Bell, April 16, 1864.

1. The First Indiana Battery will report for duty, with all material in its possession, to Brigadier General J. A. Mower, commanding First and Third Divisions, Sixteenth Army Corps, for assignment to the First Division.

By order of Brigadier General A. J. Smith:

J. HOUGH,
Assistant Adjutant-General.
