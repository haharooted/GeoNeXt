CAMP BABBITT,
 Near Visalia, Tulare County, Cal., Numbers 13.
 October 31, 1862.

I. This camp is hereby named and shall hereafter be known and called Camp Babbitt, in honor of Lieutenant Colonel E. B. Babbitt, deputy quartermaster-general, U. S. Army, Department of the Pacific.

* * * * * *

By order:

GEO. S. EVANS,

Lieutenant-Colonel Second Cavalry California Volunteers, Commanding
