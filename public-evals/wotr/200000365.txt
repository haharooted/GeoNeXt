SPECIAL ORDERS,
 HDQRS. DEPARTMENT OF THE GULF. Numbers 20.
 New Orleans, La., January 23, 1864

* * * * * *

4. Brigadier-General Dwight will relieve the Eighty-third Regiment Ohio Volunteers from duty at Fort Jackson, and will order it to proceed at once to New Orleans, La. The commanding officer, upon his arrival, will report to Major General E. O. C. Ord, commanding the Thirteenth Army Corps.

* * * * *

By command of Major-General Banks:

GEO. B. DRAKE,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
